Seismic Shaker

Seismic Shaker rattles your browser whenever a strong earthquake occurs near you.

It works by polling the U.S. Geological Survey's feed of recent earthquakes and checking if there is any activity above M2.5 within 500km of your current location (these values can be changed in the Add-on Manager). The locations of recent quakes will appear on this map:

If you live in an area of high earthquake risk you should familiarize yourself with National Geographic's page on Earthquake Safety Tips.

You can uninstall this add-on via the Firefox Add-ons Manager.
