Seismic Shaker v1.1

About this Add-on

Seismic Shaker rattles your browser window whenever a strong earthquake occurs near you.

This add-on hourly checks the U.S. Geological Survey's feed of recent M 2.5+ earthquakes hourly and shakes your active browser window should an earthquake happen anywhere within 500km of your current location.

If you can't wait for Mother Earth to get angry, you can simulate an earthquake event.

Earthquake Safety

If you live in an area of high earthquake risk you should familiarize yourself with National Geographic's page on Earthquake Safety Tips.

Uninstall

You can uninstall this add-on via the Firefox Add-ons Manager.
